Bingo - Game of luck & Strategy

Bingo is a game of luck with numbers & Strategy ! Let's Try your luck & Bingo !

Bingo is game full of fun & strategy.Bingo is a chase game between you and PC. Are you smart enough to beat PC and complete your Bingo before PC does ? If you think so, then Let's play Bingo !. Beat PC and prove you Luck with strategy. How to Play In Bingo a Grid (5x5) of random numbers will be shown to the user and PC from 1 to 25. User can select one number at a time. Number selected by you and PC will be highlighted on both number boards(your's and PC's).You have to select numbers in such a sequence that you have 5 adjacent selected numbers either horizontally, vertically or diagonally on your board. Each time you make such sequence of 5 selected numbers one character of Bingo will be granted to you and you have to repeat this process until you get all of the Bingo characters before PC does.
